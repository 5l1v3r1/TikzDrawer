Tutorial

Hey welcome!
To draw a new node, simply click anywhere on the canvas.
To select a node, right click on it. A box will appear allowing you to change the properties of the node.
To draw an edge, drag a line from one node to another using the right mouse button. You can bend the edge by dragging it with the left mouse button.
To delete a node, select it and press delete or drag it to the red part of the canvas.
